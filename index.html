About Us

-- Putting the   Fast   in Fast Food --

Maxcap is a mobile and web development that allows users to keep an eye on the wait lines of local franchises.
Revolutionizing the fast food industry and it's facets is our purpose.

"I can definitely see the value of a service of this sort. Maxcap epitomizes time conservation, convenience and

- Casey Li, business magnate, philantropist, living legend, and advocate for a better future.

Our Method

Hardware Installation






Cutting edge laser technology sensors are installed at the entrances, that accurately determine the amount of people entering and leaving the store.

Data Storage






This data is uploaded and stored in our secure databases. The information is then processed rigorously and returns very accurately approximated wait times.

Live Updates

The wait times are then updated in real-time on the user interface of the franchise's app. This information is then accessible by the user in a simplified format.
